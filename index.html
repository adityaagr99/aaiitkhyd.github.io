About SMC

Startup Master Class is an initiative of IITK Alumni Association that came into existence 4 years ago. To achieve its mandate of fostering and nurturing budding entrepreneurs, It brings together startups, mentors and investors to build interactions and strengthen the network within the entrepreneurial community.

Startup Master Class Platform is a fully volunteer run, not for profit network of passionate individuals and institutions (alumni of PAN IIT network) combining their skills and resources in support of the activities to promote entrepreneurship.

Main Stage

Hear it from the experienced lot. Their stories will help entrepreneur in all stages to assess growth and scale to IPO stage. Learn from the innovators who have left a mark. The problems that they faced and the way they solved them. Also, understand the intellection of the investors who decide to invest money and resources in a raw idea.

  • Early, Growth and Scale Stages
  • Q&A session for the inquisitive minds
  • Fireside Chat

Each startup/participant will get 3 to 5 minutes with at least 3 VCs, incubators or accelerators. We will also provide a platform to startups for pitching in front of a 7-8 VC panel at once.

One-on-One

Face to face discussion with several Angels, VCs and CEOs. Facing dilemma whether or not to startup? Facing initial challenges in your startup? Pitch your idea and get mentored from the masters.

  • Mentorship
  • Informal Pitch
  • Several Angel Investors, VCs and CEOs

One-on-ones are one of the most popular attractions at Startup Master Class that allow entrepreneurs in various stages of starting up a new venture to have a 10-15 minute face to face discussion with investors, industry veterans and mentors.

SMC Select

Are you ready to dive in and take a few laps with the sharks? Pitch your business in front of a panel of VCs. Focus on how to nail your startup pitch.

  • Get evaluated by a panel of investors
  • Only top few startups will get to pitch

SMC Startup Select is open for all. Selected start-ups will be asked to attend and present in our Startup Select session on 17th February, 2018 in front of an eminent panel of judges from the most sought-after mentors and investment firms.

The top 3 winners will get a one hour mentorship session to present their ideas in detail to the VCs and discuss about funding.

  • The first 50 start-ups who register will get an edge in getting short-listed.
  • Top 10 startups will get to pitch
  • Last date to apply for SMC Startup Select is 4th February 2018
