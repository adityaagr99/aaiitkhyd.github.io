About SMC

Startup Master Class is an initiative of IITK Alumni Association that came into existence 4 years ago. To achieve its mandate of fostering and nurturing budding entrepreneurs, It brings together startups, mentors and investors to build interactions and strengthen the network within the entrepreneurial community.

Startup Master Class Platform is a fully volunteer run, not for profit network of passionate individuals and institutions (alumni of PAN IIT network) combining their skills and resources in support of the activities to promote entrepreneurship.

One-on-One

One-on-ones are one of the most popular attractions at Startup Master Class that allow entrepreneurs in various stages of starting up a new venture to have a 10-15 minute face to face discussion with investors, industry veterans and mentors. SMC Central being organized at IIT Kanpur this time brings an additional opportunity to interact with faculty and researchers at the cutting edge of advanced technologies such as cyber security, micro/nano fabrication, algorithms, image processing, biotech and flexible electronics to name a few. If you would like to brainstorm your business model, revenue model, product pipeline, strategy to enter market, company culture, commercialize academic research, setup a research collaboration and/or gain from the wisdom of eminent folks.

SMC Select

SMC Select claims the most spectacular moments during the SMC event. We are attracting start-ups with the best business models and innovative solutions with proven traction and a huge potential. We choose the best of these best start-ups to pitch their business ideas in front of renowned investors. We are immensely proud to have our judging panel decked with Accel, Sequoia, Kalaari, Matrix, Helion, Jungle Ventures, IAN, Angel investors, Serial entrepreneurs and HNIs from USA. SMC Select is the platform to give you the right momentum and spring forward.
